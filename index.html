ABOUT US

At NuZine, our ethos is founded in the belief that our involvement in a project can be born at any point in the process. And, although the process should always remain the same, not all projects are created equal.

Projects may follow a relatively straight course, but sometimes they zig and zag, and we pride ourselves on being able to jump in anywhere, or anytime we are required.

With team expertise in Design, Project Management and DOB Processes, our cooperative approach to projects allows for team flexibility, clients having options, and needs being fulfilled by a single point of contact.

MEET THE FOUNDER

LAURA REDDY
CEO FOUNDER

Where did you grow up?
Hicksville, NY

What did you want to be when you grew up?
Designer or Solid Gold dancer

What pointed you down this career path?
Not sure, maybe my parents. My Mom was house proud, my Dad was an Electrician, 2 of my Brothers are carpenters.

Any early connections? No, just my oldest brother bringing me on job sites at a very young age.

How did you get started in the business? My first degree is in Fashion and during the marketing part of the program I realized how much I like to create and build Environments.

I worked as a retail merchandiser and floral designer for the next couple of years. Itching for more of a challenge I moved to Los Angeles where I was a free lance artist for special events, film, television and theatre.

This was such an incredible time; I worked on so many amazing projects and learned so much. This chapter was forced to an end due to the North Ridge earthquake. I returned to New York and went back to school for Interior Design at NYIT where I graduated with honors. After college I worked at a small Architectural firm, freelancing on the side and looking for the next step.

I entered into design competitions with two colleagues to find out they were in the same position; from here NY3 Design Group was born in 2002.

I secured the majority shares of the company, obtained WMBE certification, rebranded the firm, sold my shares in 2012 and started NuZine.

What are the many roles you have worked in throughout your career?
As every business owner can attest, I have had to be everything from CEO, Head Designer.
